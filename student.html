📝 How to attend today's exam

  • Today's test has 0 question(s). The exact time limit for every question is displayed below.
  • Read every question carefully. Equations, mathematical expressions and tables will be displayed exactly as configured by the administrator.
  • Every question has its own countdown timer. Submit your answer before the timer reaches zero.
  • Selecting an option will immediately submit your answer. Once submitted, the answer cannot be changed.
  • The system will only save your answer. Correct or wrong answer indication will not be displayed during the test.
  • If the timer reaches zero without selecting an answer, the question will be recorded as unattempted with 0 marks.
  • One-strike violation policy: Any prohibited activity will immediately terminate today's test.
  • Prohibited activities include: tab switching, application switching, browser minimization, exiting full-screen mode, notification interaction, losing browser focus, copy, cut, paste, right-click, print shortcut, save-page shortcut, view-source shortcut or developer-tools activity.
  • If any violation is detected, today's test will be permanently marked as completed.
  • After a violation, you cannot reopen or continue the same test.
  • Answers submitted before the violation will remain saved. Marks already obtained from those submitted answers will also remain saved.
  • The current unanswered question and all remaining questions will not be submitted after a violation.
  • Correct answers can be viewed from the Answer Key page only after the administrator releases the answer key.
